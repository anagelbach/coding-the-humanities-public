Coding the Humanities

in the humanities use a wide range of specialized tools, each of which only covers a small part of their research. Coding, on the other hand, is a toolmaking tool. It gives you the power to write precise instruments that can cover your entire workflow. In this workshop, we teach humanists how to build custom tools or adjust existing ones to their specific needs.
